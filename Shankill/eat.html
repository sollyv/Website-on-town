Eating in Shankill

A place with endless natural beauty and captivating history.
It is located in the south-east of the historic County Dublin, close to the border with County Wicklow

Bradys of Shankill

Bradys of Shankill is an award winning restaurant and bar. It is known as one of South Dublin's leading go-to destinations for morning coffee, afternoon tea or evening dining and is world renowned amongst tourists across the globe. The pub is very famous, winning the award for the 'Best Local Pub' in Ireland at the 2012 Sky Bar Awards, a prize they were again nominated for in 2016. Brady's also won the 2015 national prizes for 'Best Bar Food' and the 'Best Charity Event'.It’s a must visit when in Shankill.
