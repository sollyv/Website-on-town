Getting Here

Getting to Shankill has never been easier.Browse through this page to find your best route for getting to Shankill.


Bus Services


Dublin Bus


  • Route 7b: Mountjoy Sq to Shankill

  • Route 145: Heuston Rail Stn-Shankill-Bray

  • Route 155: IKEA-Shankill-Bray

  • Route 84/84a: Blackrock-Shankill-Newcastle

Aircoach


  • Route 702: Dublin Airport-Shankill-Greystones

Dart Sevices


Malahide/Howth-Shankill-Bray/Greystones
Shankill is the third last stop on the Dart from Connolly to Greystones.

The train is probably the handiest way to get to Shankill, offering stunning views along the way as you travel through Blackrock and Dun-Laoghaire. Shankill Dart Station is located at Rathsallagh Bridge,Shankill (5 minutes walk from Shankill Village)

Luas


The closest LUAS stop (Green Line) is at Cherrywood/Bridesglen ,with regular connections bringing passengers into the heart of the city (St. Stephen's Green) in approx. 30 minutes.
